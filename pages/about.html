About This Project

Purpose of the Capstone

To produce a practical, research-based blueprint that translates academic findings into steps a new entrepreneur can implement to build a monetizable brand.

The research synthesizes peer-reviewed literature and industry data into an accessible five-part framework designed for early-stage founders.

Why This Topic

This topic aligns with career goals in digital entrepreneurship and content-driven business models.

The research also supports a planned transition from hobby projects to a revenue-generating venture — making it both an academic exercise and a personal roadmap.

Academic Note

About the Research

This website presents findings from a high school capstone research project. All sources are cited in APA format and listed in full on the Essay page. The research synthesizes peer-reviewed academic articles, industry white papers, and empirical studies published post-2020.

This project does not constitute legal, financial, or professional business advice. It is an academic synthesis intended for educational purposes.
